About us

Do you like to travel? Are you tired of sitting at home and looking at beautiful pictures of amazing landscapes, impressive mountains, deep and mysterious seas and oceans, wide fields and huge cities from your phone screen? Or you can't decide what to visit first: the Eiffel Tower in Paris or the Statue of Liberty in New York? We will help you with all of that! Plan your trips, check out different routes and discover interesting places. Make your trip easier and more memorable. See the whole world with Global!

How do I start?

Are you ready to start using Global? All you need to do is fill out the form in the Contact tab and click Submit. Here you go. Welcome to Global!

I don't know where to go:(

Can't choose where to go first? Then visit the Trips tab or check out our tops in the Stats tab. We're sure you'll find something interesting. Good luck!
